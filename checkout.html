Terms & Conditions
1. Order & Payment

By placing an order on Global Bunjang, you agree to purchase the item at the listed price in USD. All payments are processed securely. Prices are subject to exchange rate fluctuations and may vary slightly at the time of settlement.

2. Proxy

A Proxy is a third-party service that purchases items from Korean sellers on your behalf and ships them internationally. Your selected Proxy handles purchase, warehousing, and international shipping. International shipping fees, handling fees, and service policies (returns, consolidation, etc.) are set by each Proxy — review the respective Proxy's page for full terms.

3. Customs & Duties

Import taxes, customs duties, and any related fees are the sole responsibility of the recipient. Global Bunjang is not liable for delays or additional costs incurred due to customs procedures in the destination country.

4. Order Cancellation

Orders may not be cancelled once the seller has confirmed the transaction. If cancellation is required prior to seller confirmation, please contact support promptly. Refund eligibility depends on the status of the order at the time of the request.

5. Returns & Refunds

As a secondhand marketplace, all sales are generally final. Returns are accepted only in cases where the item received significantly differs from the listing description. Claims must be submitted within 3 days of delivery.

6. Personal Information

Your personal information, including name and contact details, may be shared with the Proxy solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.
